My story
The Beginning
Where it all started
Started the CA articleship at 18 — three years of reconciling books, filing returns, and sitting across auditors who'd seen everything. Cleared the CA Final in November 2017, one of India's hardest professional exams. That was the foundation. Everything since has been building on it — from a Big4 audit floor in Bengaluru to a client site in Abu Dhabi, from Meesho's hypergrowth chaos to restating financials for a potential IPO at CHEP India. Today, as the first finance hire at ShopOS, the journey has come full circle — back to building from zero, this time with a decade of hard-won clarity.
